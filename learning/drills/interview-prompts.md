# Interview Prompts (Project-Specific)

Answer each in 60-120 seconds.

1. Walk me through this system end-to-end for a user query.
2. Why separate admin and user frontends?
3. Where is tenant isolation enforced, and what are likely leak points?
4. Why use schema-first validation and what does `parsed.data` buy you?
5. Describe a failure that causes 502 in user portal and your triage sequence.
6. What are current stubs/placeholders, and how would you productionize them?
7. What are p50 vs p95 latency, and how do they shape design choices?
8. Why asynchronous ingest instead of synchronous ingest?
9. What is the role of Durable Objects in this architecture?
10. Which parts are most risky for client launch and why?
